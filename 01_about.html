ABOUT

At the beginning of my post-secondary study, I simply imaged that I would like to work within some kind of design fields like graphic design and entered the School of Interactive Arts and Technology. Through working with different teams and playing different roles, I have explored my abilities and interests. Then, I landed at the place between programming and design where I find a lot of fun. Finally, I realized that game development brings me even more fun and game industry is the place where I eager to enter as a gameplay programmer.

Contributed in various projects, I was usually in charge of low/high-fidelity prototype through programming, application/software design following object oriented model and communicating with other designers to solve problems. I am comfortable with programming in C++, Java and JavaScript and have experienced game developments using Unity 3D . In addition, I am constantly learning and practicing unfamiliar game development tools and engines such as ActionScript and Unreal Engine.
